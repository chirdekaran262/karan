About PEGuard: Malware Detection Made Simple

PEGuard is a specialized web application designed to detect potential malware threats within Portable Executable (PE) files. Utilizing advanced machine learning techniques, our platform enables you to quickly and accurately identify suspicious files before they pose a risk.
